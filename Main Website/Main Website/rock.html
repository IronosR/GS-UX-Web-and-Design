Greta Van Fleet – Starcatcher

Greta Van Fleet threads the familiar line between extravagant guitars and crystalline wails on their third album Starcatcher.
Their previous album, The Battle At Garden’s Gate, saw the group continue their open love-letter to classic rock, whilst touching upon socially-progressive issues.
This time around, Greta Van Fleet does not stray far away from their signature sound. Rather, the group is drawn towards aesthetics, delving deep into the allure of nature, a universe which would come to inhabit Starcatcher.
Produced by Dave Cobb, Starcatcher, likens its predecessors, full of unapologetic self-expression and bejewelled jumpsuits.
